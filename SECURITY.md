# Security Policy

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in AdKit, email contact@adkit.so.

Please include the affected component or version, steps to reproduce, expected impact, and any relevant logs or screenshots.

We will acknowledge valid reports within 72 hours and provide updates as we investigate.

Please do not publicly disclose the issue until we have confirmed a fix or mitigation.

When validating a report, avoid accessing, modifying, or sharing data that is not your own.
