# Privacy Summary

AdKit connects to user-authorized ad accounts on connected ad platforms.

The plugin may access ad account data, campaign configurations, creative metadata, and performance metrics needed to perform requested actions.

Requests are processed by AdKit's MCP server at https://mcp.adkit.so.

The plugin itself does not store OAuth credentials. Credentials are handled by the platform's OAuth flow.

Stored data may include account identifiers, operation metadata, draft configurations, and records needed to provide account management features.

Ad account data, campaign settings, and performance metrics may be passed through to complete the requested command and are not stored by the plugin unless required for the requested operation.

The plugin does not include analytics or tracking.

Full privacy policy: https://adkit.so/privacy
